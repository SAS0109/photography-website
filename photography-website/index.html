About Me

Navaneet Unnikrishnan

Navaneeth Unnikrishnan is an Astro-Landscape photographer from Kerala.I represents various brands including Sony, Gitzo, LowePro & Columbia Sportswear. I started photography as a hobby using a basic DSLR and later moved into Astro-Landscape Photography. I then began shooting deep-sky objects using telescopes and has numerous images published in international magazines including National Geographic, Digital Camera Magazine, Air India, and websites including BBC Earth, Huffington Post, BuzzFeed, Space.com, Earth & Sky.I am a self-taught photographer and has learned photography through experimenting and independent study.
